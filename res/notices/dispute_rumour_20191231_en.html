Statements on recent rumours about LITEX



Dear LITEX community friends, due to various rumours about LITEX appearing on the social medias recently, LITEX Foundation hereby declares as follows:

     1. LITEX team is very stable, and not been "filed" or "caught", the news is definitely false;

     2. The technical upgrade and maintenance of the project are as usual, and the rumour "the project is left unattended and the server is shut down" is false too;

     3. LITEX official twitter and facebook accounts have been operating normally, and the project progress will be updated as usual.

LITEX Foundation strongly condemn these irresponsible remarks, and remind everyone to be rational and wary of false statements and false news!
